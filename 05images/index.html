Class Selection

The Main Classes

There are three main classes to choose from to start, which also each contain subclasses. They are:

Warlock
A scholar of the D2 universe, powerful in all the magic and abilities that guardians wield
Hunter
A rogue type character and a scout, excelling at stealth and ranged damage output
Titan
Unbreakable walls of Destiny 2, Titans can hold the line with big melee damage and increased resistances

While each main class has an over arching identity their subclasses can further refine the way that you play.
Destiny 2 is all about the player's control over their own experience
The best way to learn more is to hear it from Bungie but you can find all the subclasses listed below.

Warlock
  • Voidwalker: An "energy vampire" that regains ability energy through damage.
  • Dawn Blade: A solar class that has air superiority bonuses and great healing.
  • Storm Trance: An arc class that specializes in chain lightning and damage resistance.
Hunter
  • Nightstalker: A void stealth class with an archer motif and great debuffs to support your team.
  • Arc Staff: Arc melee focused class, punch dodge and repeat for chain lightning and healing.
  • Gunslinger: Summon a golden gun made from solar energy to do unmatched damage.
Titan
  • Sentinel: Protect your team and debuff the enemy with this void shield class.
  • Hammer of Sol: Solar class with a blacksmith theme, summon flaming hammers to do damage and stand in the resulting hearthfire for healing.
  • Striker: Arc subclass with a melee focus, huge damage bonuses and healing when surrounded.
